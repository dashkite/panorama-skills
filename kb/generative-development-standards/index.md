# Generative Development Standards

This guide outlines standards and protocols for generative AI systems and human developers collaborating within the Panorama ecosystem.

## Collaboration Philosophy
In a generative codebase, human developers and AI systems pair-program interactively. To maximize efficiency:
- **Scannable Indentation**: Logic flow is laid out via structural block indentation rather than early returns.
- **Context Budgeting**: Files and functions are kept modular and small to fit into token limit parameters.
- **Communication Discipline**: High-level plans and session logs are documented at each stage of work.
